Cheese Fridge

Ken Sparling

I have a little white bar fridge that I keep down in the basement.

I have had it since I went to university.

That was, like, fifty years ago.

I keep cheese in it.

Cracker Barrel.

Old Cheddar.

I have about twenty-five bars of the stuff.

I buy it when it’s on sale.

Then I let it age down there in the little fridge.

Kitty calls it my cheese fridge.

She hates the thing.

I always like to say that everything I do is designed to make Kitty happy.
